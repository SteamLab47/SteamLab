About Our Lab

The Hi-Tech MCS STEM Laboratory is a premier research facility dedicated to advancing Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics education and innovation. Established in 2023, our lab serves as a hub for cutting-edge research and hands-on learning experiences.

Spanning over 5,000 square feet, our facility houses state-of-the-art equipment and provides a collaborative environment for students, researchers, and industry partners to work on transformative projects.

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Laboratory
  • 24/7 Access for Research Teams
  • Industry-Academia Collaboration Hub
  • International Research Partnerships

Key Features

  • High-Speed Internet (10 Gbps)
  • Cloud Computing Infrastructure
  • Advanced Security Systems
  • Climate-Controlled Environment
  • Sustainable Energy Sources
  • Remote Lab Access Capability
  • Research Data Management System
  • Collaborative Workspaces
